Made by everyone, for everyone.

Ariadne is a localised mesh network communication infrastructure. By operating independently of large systems, Ariadne allows for local connection without the need for a provider. The network is open-source - ensuring transparency - and community-operated - meaning only you and your neighbours have the power to keep everybody connected.
